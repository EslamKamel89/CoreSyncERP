# Roles & Permissions (Draft Plan)

The ERP includes four main roles. Permissions will be expanded as modules develop.

---

## Admin

**Permissions:**

-   Manage roles & permissions
-   Manage company settings
-   Access all modules
-   Override restrictions for testing/troubleshooting

---

## HR Manager

**Permissions:**

-   Manage departments & positions
-   Manage employees (create/update/archive)
-   Manage attendance & leave requests
-   Run payroll cycles

---

## Inventory Manager

**Permissions:**

-   Manage products, units, and categories
-   Manage warehouses
-   Create purchase orders & receive stock
-   Create sales orders & ship stock
-   Perform stock adjustments

---

## Accountant

**Permissions:**

-   Manage chart of accounts
-   Create & approve journal entries
-   View ledger, trial balance, and P&L
-   Review integrations from HR & Inventory
