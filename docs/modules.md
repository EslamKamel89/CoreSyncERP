# Modules Overview

This document lists all ERP modules and their responsibilities.

---

## Core Module

**Responsibilities:**

-   Authentication & authorization (roles and permissions)
-   Application layout, navigation, and localization
-   Company profile & app-wide settings
-   Notifications, toasts, and shared UI components

---

## HR Module

**Responsibilities:**

-   Departments, positions, and organizational structure
-   Employee master data
-   Attendance & leave management
-   Payroll calculation and approval
-   Integration with accounting for payroll posting

---

## Inventory Module

**Responsibilities:**

-   Products, categories, units of measure
-   Warehouses & stock levels
-   Stock movements (in/out/adjustments)
-   Purchase orders & receiving stock
-   Sales orders & shipping stock
-   Stock valuation (Weighted Average)

---

## Accounting Module

**Responsibilities:**

-   Chart of accounts
-   Journal entries & double-entry engine
-   Ledger, trial balance, and financial statements
-   Integration with HR (payroll) and inventory (COGS, inventory valuation)
